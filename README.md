🧠 ChainTheory-AI v3.0 (Alpha)
Understanding How Students Think — Not Just What They Answer

ChainTheory-AI is an educational reasoning engine designed to analyze student explanations, identify concept understanding, detect misconceptions, and provide actionable feedback for both students and teachers. Unlike traditional answer checkers, ChainTheory-AI focuses on student thinking rather than simply marking answers as correct or incorrect.
